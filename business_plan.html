STEM Bot's Business Plan

Operations

The STEM bot is a unique machine that helps you with sorting and cleaning up garbage in public places like in school a campus.
It is a helpful device that will help the environment and the workers that are working on the school campus.
One reason why STEM bot is unique is that there aren’t any other robots that has these abilities.
Another reason that STEM bot is unique is that it can work anytime.
The school can’t hire workers to clean up messes on weekends or other non-school times.
The STEM bot can work and clean up day or night. The STEM bot makes life so much easier.

Executive Summary

STEM Bot is an eco-friendly robot that picks up trash, sorts trash, and takes trash out. STEM bot stands for Sorting,Technological, Environmental, Mobile robot.
The features of the robot include two arms, five to eight feet tall, sorts trash into landfill, compost,and recycl, five feet can extend all the way to eight feet, STEM bot has wheels so it can move around.
There is also an app that controls the robot when you’re not there.
STEM bot has a camera, and is made to be used for schools. Our company was founded on the twenty-second of July in 2019.The members of our team consists of Doyoon Kim, Hamza Raza, Krish Nalekar, Isaac Ho, and Trish Chettypally.
